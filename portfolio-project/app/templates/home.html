About Me

Hello, here I will tell you something about me.

hey, I am Stijn Septer

Hello, my name is Stijn Septer, and I am a Back-end web developer. You may have seen that the front-end of this website is minimal and that is because, I gain way more joy if I am working on the backend of a website. This is why I made this portfolio in Django and saved all of the data in the project and in the slideshow in de Django database. I know that doesn’t really make any sense for a portfolio but, it was fun challenge.

Another small problem was that it is difficult to host a Django project on many serves. So, that is why I decided to get it a raspberry pi to host this website. Another challenge for me was the front-end, this is for the portfolio and may other project that I have work on a bit of a struggle but, I got something that works well enough. Hopefully you like the project that I have made and if you want to see more, you can always check my GitHub.
